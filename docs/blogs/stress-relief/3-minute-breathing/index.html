3-Minute Breathing Exercise to Calm Your Mind

Feeling overwhelmed or tense? This simple breathing exercise can help you reset your mind in just 3 minutes ‚Äî no special tools or space required.

Step 1: Find a Comfortable Seat

Sit upright in a chair or cross-legged on the floor. Rest your hands on your lap or knees and gently close your eyes if you're comfortable.

Step 2: Inhale Slowly

Breathe in through your nose for a count of four. Feel your chest and belly rise as you fill your lungs with air.

Step 3: Hold and Exhale

Hold the breath for a count of two, then exhale gently through your mouth for a count of six. Let your shoulders relax as you release the air.

Step 4: Repeat

Repeat this cycle ‚Äî inhale (4), hold (2), exhale (6) ‚Äî for 3 minutes. Focus your attention on the rhythm of your breath.

This exercise can be done during a break, before a meeting, or anytime you need a moment to reset. Try it daily for lasting calm.
